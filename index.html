Curriculum vitae

My research focuses on understanding the importance of animals in forest ecosystems. My current research includes (1) measuring and modelling the response of forest soils to non-native earthworms, (2) modelling the habitat requirements of species at risk in Atlantic Canada, and (3) modelling the interactions between moose and forests on the island of Newfoundland. My overarching objective is to help us understand why animals live where they do and their impact on forest ecosystems.
